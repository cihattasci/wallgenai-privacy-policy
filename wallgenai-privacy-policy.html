Privacy Policy

WallGenAI - AI Wallpaper Generator
Last Updated: December 25, 2024

1. Introduction

WallGenAI ("app", "service", "we" or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Data: In-app activities, feature usage, error reports
  • Technical Information: IP address, device identifiers, network information

2.2 User-Provided Information

  • AI Prompts: Text descriptions you enter to create wallpapers
  • Account Information: Account data created through Supabase authentication
  • Purchase Information: Credit packages and in-app purchase history

3. How We Use Information

  • Service Provision: AI wallpaper generation, gallery management
  • Personalization: Improving user experience
  • Technical Support: Troubleshooting and performance optimization
  • Security: Fraud prevention and security maintenance
  • Legal Obligations: Meeting legal requirements

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 AI Services

DALL-E 3 (OpenAI): Your prompts are sent to OpenAI's DALL-E 3 service to create wallpapers. OpenAI's own privacy policy applies.

4.2 Backend Services

  • Supabase: User authentication, data storage, and real-time database
  • Google Play/App Store: In-app purchase processing

4.3 Analytics and Crash Reporting

  • Anonymous usage data may be collected to monitor app performance
  • Crash reports are used to resolve technical issues

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Retention Period

  • User Accounts: Until account deletion
  • Wallpapers: Until user deletion
  • Purchase Records: As required by legal obligations
  • Log Data: Maximum 90 days

5.2 Security Measures

  • Data is stored encrypted
  • Secure HTTPS connections are used
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Regular security updates

6. User Rights

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: You can delete your account and data
  • Right to Portability: You can request a copy of your data
  • Right to Object: You can object to data processing

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

8. International Data Transfer

Your data may be processed in countries where our service providers are located. These transfers are made with appropriate security measures in place.

9. Cookies and Tracking

  • Local storage is used to improve app performance
  • Device storage is used to save user preferences
  • No third-party tracking services are used

10. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes. The updated policy will be effective from the date of publication.

11. Contact

If you have questions about our privacy policy, you can contact us:

  • Email: privacy@wallgenai.com
  • In-App: Settings > Support > Privacy
  • Web: https://wallgenai.com/privacy

12. Legal Basis

The legal basis for processing your personal data:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to provide the service
  • Legitimate Interest: Service improvement and security
  • Consent: Marketing and optional features
  • Legal Obligation: Meeting legal requirements
